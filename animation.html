Serenity Animation

In this animation, I wanted to capture a calm and peaceful feeling. The clouds move slowly across the sky while the trees sway softly, almost like they are breathing. The relaxing music ties everything together, turning a simple scene into a quiet moment of serenity.

Watch Animation

Why This Animation Matters

This animation matters because it shows how simple motion and sound can change how we feel. There are no fast cuts or heavy effects. Instead, the slow clouds, gentle trees, and soft music work together to create a space that feels quiet and safe. It is a reminder that even small, simple scenes can carry a strong emotion.

What I Learned

Working on this project taught me how timing, smooth movement, and audio all have to support the same feeling. I learned that if the clouds move too fast or the music is too loud, the calm emotion breaks. By adjusting the speed, easing, and sound levels, I was able to make the whole piece feel more peaceful and controlled.

This project also showed me that planning the emotion first makes it easier to choose the right motion, sound, and pacing for the final animation.
